WP ivoox

Plugin to insert an audio from ivoox.com

It's a simple plugin to embed the ivoox.com player in a post or page of a WordPress Blog

The shortcode looks like this:

[ivoox]http://www.ivoox.com/lgds-la-guarida-del-sith-3x16-thor-steven-audios-mp3_rf_2582787_1.html[/ivoox]

The URL must be the audio URL from the browser address bar.
